Sphagetti

Description

Yes, tofu sphagetti is a thing. It's a very delicious thing that you should make immediately.

Ingridients

  • Marianara Sauce
  • Sphagetti
  • Tofu
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Arugula
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Take pasta in the boilng water for 8 minutes
  3. Minced garlic and then put garlic and tofu in olive oil
  4. Fry the tofu and garlic until it is crispy
  5. Combine the spaghetti and garlic and tofu with mariana sauce
  6. Mix with arugula while the sphagett is hot then put parmesan to your liking
  7. Bon Appetit!
