Braised-Kabocha

Description

Cooked in savory dashi broth seasoned with soy sauce and sake, this classic Japanese Simmered Kabocha makes a great healthy side dish that is chock-full of nutrients.

Ingredients

  • Kabocha
  • Japanese multipurpose sauce
  • water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the skin off of kabocha then cut it into cubes.
  2. Boil the water with multipurpose sauce
  3. Put kabocha in the pot
  4. Leave in there for 15minutes.
  5. Add salt and pepper for your liking
  6. Bon Appetit!
